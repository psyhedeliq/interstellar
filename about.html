About Us

The company was founded in 2002 to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets.

What We Do

InterStellar has gained worldwide attention for a series of historic milestones. It is the only private company capable of returning a spacecraft from low Earth orbit, which it first accomplished in 2010. The company made history again in 2012 when its spacecraft became the first commercial spacecraft to deliver cargo to and from the International Space Station.

InterStellar successfully achieved the historic first reflight of an orbital class rocket in 2017, and the company now regularly launches flight-proven rockets. In 2018, InterStellar began launching the world’s most powerful operational rocket by a factor of two. As one of the world’s fastest growing providers of launch services, InterStellar has secured over 60 missions to its manifest, representing over $6 billion on contract. These include commercial satellite launches as well as US government missions.
